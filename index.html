My Projects

Custom Itineray Recommender and Generator App



This dissertation project presents a concept of a mobile application where the system takes in the users trip preferences and then suggests places to visit along with logically sorting them in a customisable itinerary. This project provides a solution to -
1. Existing recommendation resources are too many and generic and do not cater to the traveler's indivdual needs
2. Inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated information present
3. Requiring an itinerary optimsed to make the best use of the available days and time.

Indoor Navigation Concept App



This personal project is an attempt to target navigation in complex indoor spaces such airports and shopping malls. This app provides a solution to -
1. Cluttered information map boards
2. Tackling the language barrier on sign boards in new countries
3. Being unaware of time taken to reach a destination in time sentive situations
4. A quick glance of all the stores and service present in a large indoor space.

Skincare Concept App



With the rise of skincare in the beauty industry, this concept app helps users analyse their facial skin's health and also assits them in finding the right proudct catering to their needs. The app provides a solution to -

UI Redesign of a Website



This project focuses on the interface elements of a website that impact the user's overall experience. I have analysed the home page of an existing website catering to pets and their services and identified issues with its consistency, information architecture, usability, visual hierarchy, etc. Secondly, I have redesigned its home page, focusing solely on the visual elements to give a more harmonious feel to the page.
